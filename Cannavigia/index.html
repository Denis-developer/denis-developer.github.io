Integrated Compliance Regulations

The cannabis industry compliance regulations are constantly changing, and keeping track of them is essential. With these in mind, we have developed the CANNAVIGIA software, which includes a cultivation module in line with the World-wide Standard for Good Agricultural Practices (GlobalGAP), Good Agricultural and Collection Practices (GACP), and Swiss Certified Cannabis (SCC) to ensure seamless compliance with these standards in your operations.

Automated Certificate of Analysis (CoA)

Certificates of Analysis or CoAs are Quality Assurance documents that confirm product compliance with regulations and specification. CoAs usually contain real results received from the quality control test performed on a group of products. Our automated CoA can improve grower's information management and sharing, material documentation, and validation with a timely and accurate collection of necessary data. Other advantages the system can give include:

Quality

Confirming that your product meets specification with an official backup document.

Сonfidence

Building customers confidence and giving you an edge over other competitors.

Audit

Ability to view specs on every lot and carry out audit/inventory control with ease.

Individual doc

Providing a customized/professional CoA document that can include your letterhead.

Reporting

Creating and sharing different reports automatically at a certain time interval (both in real-time and historical) will eliminate the manual means of communicating data for product traceability and compliance with regulations.

Taking Clones

Taking clones from the mother enables you to preserve the exact genetic traits of a particular strain. Our software makes this process a lot easier and more efficient. With the cultivation tool, you can take full control of the entire process, including monitoring your yield and efficiency.

for genetic and clone providers, Cannavigia helps you to track your cloning processes. monitor your clone survival rate of each plant phenotype, keeping track of the vitality rate of the mother plants, tracking who of your workforce is cutting clones the most efficient and furthermore allowing you to take credit for your genetics as you sell them to the cultivators. your provided genetics will be shown as a part of the final product. 

As a genetics provider, one of your assets is the constant development of the next best cannabis strain. Cannavigia helps you in the selection process of your pheno hunting. by selecting new cannabis strains you need to identify the phenotype with the best attributes. If you are looking for the phenotype with the highest yield, best bud to leaf ratio, great root development, densest flower, the special flavor, powdery mildew resistance, botrytis resistance, pathogen resistance in general or just the most resin coated bud. Cannavigia helps you to log all those information in picture or note form from your smartphone. Form the analytical side you can add your terpene and cannabinoid reports to your phenotypes. Always knowing on what plant you have to focus and the ones you can remove from the selection process.

Mobile App

Managing the workforce has always been a challenge in any industry. Our user-friendly mobile app guarantees a traceable working process where staff and activities can be properly monitored and managed. You can schedule work, processes and track all real-time activities. You can also monitor if a particular plant is fed with nutrients, watered, or has enough lighting.

Every staff using the app will have to log in with their face recognition and personal ID provided during setup, making it easier to track who carried out certain activities at any given time. You have access to different user rights or permission levels – Super Admin has control over all app features, while normal users can only perform role-based or permission-based tasks.
